Resident Evil 4's Development Process

This Research Project will be spreading information on how one of the industry's highly regarded video games development process went behind the scenes.

About Resident Evil 4

Resident Evil 4 stars Leon Kennedy, a returning main character from Resident Evil 2. But instead of being a rookie cop being fighting off zombies in Raccoon City, he now works as a secret agent for the U.S. government, who has now been assigned to rescue the President's daughter. The President's daughter was caputured by a religious cult called the Los Illuminados who's leader, Osmund Saddler, has plans to take over the U.S. with a parasite called Las Plagas. The game thrives off being very cheesy and its mountain of action but since it is a horror game, it's not afarid to lean into more horror like elements like jumpscares, weird sounds and horrific emeny's.
